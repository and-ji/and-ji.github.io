About This Site

I built this site primarily as a way of honing my technical skills, and to show off a little of my work in the process. To me, my commit history is like a diary of my adventures in Web design and development—complete with the many, many mistakes I've made in the course of my self-guided journey.

Initially, I decided to build it using pure HTML and CSS, without the use of frameworks. Later, I refactored some of my old styles using Sass.

The reason I avoided frameworks is that I wanted the freedom to use my own designs, make my own mistakes, and futureproof the site against any frameworks that may one day fall out of favor.

I'm open to collaboration. If you like my style, please feel free to reach out.
