About

Andres Jauregui is a design leader with 15+ years of experience building better products by driving transformative systems and processes that yield measureable results.

He has worked at Forbes, where he built the company's design system and founded design operations; the Huffington Post, and AOL, among other companies.

He enjoys collaborating with design, product and engineering partners to build efficient, scalable, and performance-enhancing solutions to complex problems.

Based in greater Portland, Andres is inspired by the freedom he finds in Maine's woods and waters, his friends and colleagues, and his family of four.

Andres loves watching and playing soccer, anything science fiction or fantasy related, and spending time with his two wonderful kids, Isaac and Alice.
